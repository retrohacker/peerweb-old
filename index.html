Mission
Making a distributed, peer driven, internet accessible to web developers.
Technology
Peerweb is powered by torrents over WebRTC
Goals
Accessible
No knowledge of torrents or the underlying libraries necessary. Hosted solutions for creating initial seeds and linking to your content.
Resiliant
Remove single points of failure. Use peer driven solutions wherever possible.
Familiar
Seeded content should run directly in modern browsers, no need to download and install standalone software packages.
Bridged
This technology should have clean bridges between itself and the internet. This includes DNS resolution to Peerweb content as well as linking into and out of the peerweb network
